Overview
I kept getting distracted during lectures and work sessions whenever a task was mentioned in passing , submit this by the end of the day, remember to upload that later, don’t forget this discussion.

Opening Notes, Reminders, or my phone in those moments always pulled me out of focus. One quick action turned into context switching, and the original task often got lost anyway.

Turtl came from wanting a quieter way to capture intent as it happens.

It’s a macOS menu bar app designed for moments when you don’t want to switch screens. You type the task, and Turtl automatically schedules it in your calendar, no app hopping, no mental load, no interruption.

Instead of holding tasks in your head, Turtl holds them for you.
